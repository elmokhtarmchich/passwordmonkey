Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. PasswordMonkey is designed from the ground up to be completely private and secure.

No Data Storage or Transmission

All password generation happens locally in your browser on your device. We do not store, log, or transmit any passwords you create. When you refresh or close the page, the generated password is gone forever unless you have copied it elsewhere.

No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies for advertising or analytics. The only data we store locally is your preference for light or dark mode, using your browser's `localStorage`. This is purely for your convenience and does not track you in any way.
