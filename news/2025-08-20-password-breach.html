Password breach exposes millions of accounts

Published: Aug 20, 2025 | Author: Password Monkey

A recent security breach has exposed millions of accounts across major platforms. Attackers gained access through reused and weak passwords.

How to protect yourself:

  • Change your passwords immediately.
  • Use a password generator like Password Monkey to create secure, unique credentials.
  • Enable two-factor authentication where possible.
