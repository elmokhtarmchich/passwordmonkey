About PasswordMonkey

PasswordMonkey is a free, open-source tool dedicated to helping you create strong, secure, and random passwords. In an age of constant data breaches and cyber threats, a robust password is your first line of defense.

Our mission is simple: provide a powerful, easy-to-use, and completely private password generator.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We believe in privacy by design. PasswordMonkey generates all passwords directly on your device. Nothing is ever sent to our servers, and nothing is ever stored. Your passwords are yours and yours alone. For more details, please see our full Privacy Policy.

Help Us Improve

PasswordMonkey is an independent project. If you find it useful, please consider donating to help us cover server costs and continue development.
